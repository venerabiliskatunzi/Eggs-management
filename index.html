About Us

Our Story

Katunzi Eggs Management was established in 2024 with a mission to provide the highest quality eggs to our customers. We started as a small family business and have grown into a trusted supplier of eggs in the region.

Our chickens are raised in a natural environment, fed with organic feed, and cared for with the highest standards of animal welfare. This ensures that our eggs are not only delicious but also nutritious and healthy.

We take pride in our sustainable farming practices and our commitment to environmental responsibility. Our farm uses renewable energy sources and implements waste management systems to minimize our ecological footprint.
